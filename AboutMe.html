Who Am I?

Hi, I'm James, and before learning to code, my previous passion was sports which I studied through high school, college and university. It was during my degree I found out about development, where I was asked to make a website to display information about a sports supplement known as creatine. I made the page using HTML and CSS and although it was quite basic, and I didn't fully understand what I was doing, I fell in love with coding. I looked through learning resources and found Code Academy and did a few lessons which really helped me decide that this was something I wanted to pursue. Once I finished university I looked into it again and started learning in my spare time. Previous to being on the Netmatters Scion scheme, I taught myself Python and the framework Django. Since joining the Netmatters course I have been able to learn so much more. Such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript. Currently, I am also learning about algorithms in my spare time! However, if I'm not coding at my PC I will probably be training BJJ (Brazilian jiu-jitsu), reading a book or playing one of my guitars.
